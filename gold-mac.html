Some of the techniques that make this mac n cheese especially luxurious are:

  • Cooking the macaroni in stock instead of water to produce a richer flavor
  • Cougar gold cheese has a particularly deep and complex flavor. I prefer this to mixing cheeses.
  • Using saffron for an elevated taste and rich gold color
  • The blistered tomatoes add some complementary acidity that helps balance the fattiness of the cheese

Ingredients

These ingredients make ~4 modest or 2-3 generous servings. As a main dish, plan on a generous serving per person.

Ingredient Amount
Orzo pasta (dry) 3/4 cup
grape or cherry tomatoes ~8 tomatoes
Chicken (or vegetable) stock .75 quarts
Cougar gold cheese 2.5 cups
Butter 2 tbsp
Heavy cream 1/2 cup
Saffron ~1/3 tsp

Prep

  1. Break up the cheese into small pieces. This isn‚Äôt an exact science, but the smaller the pieces the faster it melts.
  2. Wash the tomatoes

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of chicken stock to a low boil. Use a wide pot or saut√© pan if available.
    1. Add 1/4 of the saffron to the stock
    2. if using unsalted chicken stock, add ~1tsp kosher salt.
  2. Add dry orzo to chicken stock and reduce temperature to medium-low.
    1. Cover and stir every minute.
    2. Only add more chicken stock in small amounts if there is little to no liquid left. The goal is to get the orzo to slightly less than al dente with almost no liquid left.
    3. Turn off the heat and keep covered.
  3. In a separate pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once it is hot, add the tomatoes.
  4. Saut√© the tomatoes for 1.5-2 minutes without moving them. Once they start to blister, rotate and repeat.
  5. Once tomatoes are twice blistered, reduce heat to medium-low. Add the cream, crumbled cougar gold cheese, and remaining saffron to the pan.
  6. Stir cheese and cream continuously until the cheese is fully melted.
  7. Once cheese is fully melted, combine cheese & tomatoes into the pot with orzo and mix together until thoroughly coated.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
